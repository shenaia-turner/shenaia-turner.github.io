BOX-LAMINATIONS

The objective of BOX-LAMINATIONS is to use the properties of different woods and the affordances of digital fabrication as design and form generators. The model of our wood is split into strips and irregular pieces were grouped from single and double curved shapes to separate the more planar pieces from the curvy pieces.
